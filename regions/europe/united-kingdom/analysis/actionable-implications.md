## For Business

If operating in UK:
- Plan for economic contraction
- Consider jurisdiction of incorporation
- Diversify revenue geographically
- Talent retention will become easier (workers seeking stability) but quality may decline (brain drain)

## Critical Question

The key personal question for UK residents:

**Are you positioned to exit if conditions deteriorate significantly?**

If the answer is no, developing that optionality is urgent. The time to acquire citizenship alternatives, portable credentials, and diversified assets is before they become obviously necessary.
