# Canada Infrastructure Pipeline

*Current capital projects and investment frameworks as of 2025.*

## Major Projects Office Pipeline

Launched in Calgary, August 2025. Over C$116 billion in capital projects across two tranches.

| Category | Projects |
|----------|----------|
| Energy | LNG terminals |
| Nuclear | Small modular reactors (SMRs) |
| Mining | Copper and nickel mines |
| Infrastructure | Transmission lines into the North |

## Build Canada Homes

- Capitalisation: C$13 billion
- Function: De-risk housing for private developers and institutional landlords
- Mechanism: Land provision, long-term low-cost finance, bulk procurement

## Canada Growth Fund

- Function: Backstops carbon-price risk
- Mechanism: 15-year offtake agreements

## Foreign Investment Agreements

### UAE Investment Protection Agreement (November 2025)

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Amount | Up to $50 billion |
| Sectors | Energy, minerals, AI infrastructure, ports |
| Protection | Investor-state dispute settlement (ISDS) |
| Duration | 15-year survival clauses |
| Partners | Canadian pension funds, Gulf sovereigns |
