# Singapore Outlook: 2026-2036

> **Classification**: Survivor
> **GDP Projection**: +2 to +4% annually
> **Confidence**: Moderate

## Core Assessment

Singapore will adapt because Singapore always adapts—but the landing zone is smaller than it once appeared. The technocratic elite understands existential threat and will execute a pivot. The question is whether the new niche is large enough to sustain Singapore's prosperity.

## Why Survivor Rather Than Winner or Declining

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Financial services exposure | High (14% GDP)—vulnerability |
| Adaptation capacity | Very high—strength |
| Infrastructure constraints | Binding—limitation |
| Governance quality | Exceptional—strength |
| Landing zone | Smaller than historical pivots |

Singapore faces real challenges but has the institutional capacity to adapt.
