# China Outlook: 2026-2036

> **Classification**: Winner
> **GDP Projection**: +4 to +6% annually
> **Confidence**: Moderate

## Core Assessment

China will pursue aggressive AI adoption regardless of social cost, backed by state capacity to suppress dissent and direct economic restructuring. This positions China as a winner in the AI transition despite significant headwinds.

| Factor | Assessment |

| State capacity | Can direct deployment |
| Social cost tolerance | Higher than democracies |
| Investment commitment | State-backed |
