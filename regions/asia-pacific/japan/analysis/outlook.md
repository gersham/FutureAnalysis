# Japan Outlook: 2026-2036

> **Classification**: Survivor
> **Growth Outlook**: Moderate
> **Confidence**: Moderate-High

## Core Assessment

Japan will manage decline with characteristic dignity. Neither leading nor collapsing, Japan's trajectory is stagnation rather than fracture. The absence of significant unassimilated populations means AI-driven unemployment will not compound into political crisis.

## What Japan Avoids

Japan's trajectory avoids the worst outcomes:

| Avoided Outcome | Why |
|-----------------|-----|
| Fracturing | Homogeneous population; social cohesion |
| Rapid decline | Already adapted to slow growth |
| Social conflict | No significant unassimilated groups |
| Institutional collapse | Strong, mature institutions |
