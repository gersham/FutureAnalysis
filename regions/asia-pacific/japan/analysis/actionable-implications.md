## Summary

Japan offers:
- High stability (very attractive in uncertain world)
- Moderate quality of life (high by global standards)
- Limited growth opportunity (already mature)
- No fracturing risk (homogeneous, strong institutions)

Appropriate for those prioritising stability over opportunity.
